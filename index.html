Sky: Children of the Light

Creatures Information
Mantas are creatures that live in both water and air, they can produce a light source to charge up the player's energy.
Dark Dragons, also known as "Krills", are large dark creatures that roams around areas around Golden Wasteland and Eye of Eden. They can attack the player with massive damage when they're under their blue light.
Dark Crabs are smaller creatures that roams in smaller areas in Golden Wasteland. They can attack the player with smaller damage and can be easily dodge from their attack.
